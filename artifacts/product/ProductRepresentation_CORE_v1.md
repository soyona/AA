# Product Identity
AA is a personal Chinese Character World Building Product for Yuanyuan.

AA helps Yuanyuan discover, understand, build, and grow through character relationships and character structures.

AA is not a literacy practice tool, electronic workbook, quiz system, or drill-based learning system.

AA transforms literacy learning from:

```text
Practice
Repeat
Memorize
```

into:

```text
Discover
Understand
Build
Create
```
---
# Product Mission
AA exists to help Yuanyuan build positive literacy experiences through sustained discovery and personal construction of a Chinese character world.

The mission is to support literacy capability growth without making the child feel that after-school character learning is only a required task, repeated exercise, or parent-enforced obligation.
---
# Product Vision
AA should become Yuanyuan's personal character world: a place where Chinese characters are not isolated items to memorize, but discoverable, connected, and meaningful building materials.

The long-term product vision is to help Yuanyuan:

- Discover character patterns
- Understand relationships between characters
- Build a personal character world
- Gain mastery and creation satisfaction
- Form positive literacy experiences

The product should optimize for continued return and world growth, not maximum practice volume in a single session.
---
# Product Core Loop
```text
Discover
↓
Understand
↓
Build
↓
Grow
↓
Discover Again
```

## Discover
Yuanyuan encounters character patterns, structures, or relationships and feels:

```text
I discovered something.
```

The intended experience is not:

```text
I completed an exercise.
```
---
## Understand
Yuanyuan begins to make sense of how characters relate to each other through structure, sound, meaning, radicals, and word formation.
---
## Build
Yuanyuan turns understanding into a personal sense of construction, connecting characters into a growing world that feels owned rather than assigned.
---
## Grow
Yuanyuan's character world expands through continued discovery and understanding, creating visible evidence of literacy growth.
---
## Discover Again
Growth creates new curiosity, leading Yuanyuan back into the next cycle of discovery.
---
# Product Experience Principles
## Principle-1: Discovery Before Completion
AA should make the primary child experience feel like discovery, not completion of assigned work.

The example pattern:

```text
木
↓
林
↓
森
```

should be experienced as:

```text
I discovered something.
```
---
## Principle-2: Construction Before Consumption
AA should frame characters as materials for building a personal world, not as content to consume or exercises to finish.
---
## Principle-3: Relationships Before Isolated Items
AA should emphasize relationships between characters and structures, so Yuanyuan can understand patterns rather than only memorize isolated characters.
---
## Principle-4: Sustained Return Before Study Volume
AA should optimize for Yuanyuan returning over time, not for longer sessions or more completed items today.
---
## Principle-5: Positive Literacy Emotion Before Pressure
AA should reduce literacy-related emotional conflict and help Yuanyuan associate Chinese characters with curiosity, mastery, and creation satisfaction.
---
# User Motivation Model
## Primary Motivation
```text
Discovery
```

Yuanyuan should want to return because there is something new to find, connect, or understand.
---
## Secondary Motivation
```text
Building
```

Yuanyuan should feel that the character world grows through personal participation and construction.
---
## Third Motivation
```text
Mastery
```

Yuanyuan should feel increasing control and confidence as character patterns and relationships become understandable.
---
## Motivation Boundaries
AA does not define motivation around:

- Rewards
- Badges
- Leaderboard
- Points
- Check-in

These are not part of this product identity.
---
# Parent Value Model
The primary parent value is not convenience.

The primary parent value is:

```text
Reduce literacy-related emotional conflict.

Build positive literacy experience.

Improve literacy capability through sustained engagement.
```

## Parent Value-1: Lower Emotional Conflict
AA should reduce the need for repeated reminders, pressure, and supervision that can turn literacy learning into family conflict.
---
## Parent Value-2: Positive Literacy Experience
AA should help the parent see that Yuanyuan can relate to Chinese characters through curiosity and construction, not only through requirement and correction.
---
## Parent Value-3: Sustained Capability Growth
AA should support literacy capability improvement through repeated engagement over time, aligned with the Solution Stage focus on long-term participation rate.
---
# Product Boundary
## In Scope
AA defines a personal Chinese Character World Building Product for Yuanyuan.

- Chinese character discovery
- Character structure understanding
- Character relationship understanding
- Personal character world building
- Positive literacy experience formation
