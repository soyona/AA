# 3. Product Identity

AA is a personal Chinese Character World Building Product for Yuanyuan.

AA helps Yuanyuan discover, understand, connect, build, and grow through Chinese character structures, character relationships, and knowledge-world construction.

AA is not:

- a literacy practice tool
- an electronic workbook
- a quiz system
- a drill-based learning system
- a check-in reward product
- a generic tutoring product

AA transforms literacy learning from:

```text
Practice
Repeat
Memorize
```

into:

```text
Discover
Understand
Build
Grow
Create New Curiosity
```

---

# 4. Product Mission

AA exists to help Yuanyuan build positive literacy experiences through sustained discovery and personal construction of a Chinese character world.

Chinese characters are the visible training ground.

Growth is the final objective.

The mission is to help Yuanyuan use literacy exploration to develop:

- character recognition
- structure understanding
- relationship reasoning
- persistence
- focus
- emotional regulation
- courage to face difficulty
- growth mindset

AA should make after-school Chinese character learning feel like a personal world that can be explored and built, not a required task enforced by parents.

---

# 5. Product Vision

AA should become Yuanyuan's personal character world.

In this world, Chinese characters are not isolated items to memorize. They are discoverable, connected, and meaningful building materials.

The long-term vision is to help Yuanyuan:

- discover character patterns
- understand relationships between characters
- build a personal knowledge world
- see growth through accumulated understanding
- return because the world can keep expanding
- experience difficulty as part of growth rather than as failure

AA should optimize for:

```text
continued return and world growth
```

not:

```text
maximum practice volume in a single session
```

# 7. Product Core Loop

AA's product loop is:

```text
Discover
↓
Understand
↓
Build
↓
Grow
↓
Discover Again
```

This is the AA-specific adaptation of the selected Minecraft Product DNA.

It preserves Minecraft-style discovery, construction, ownership, and world expansion while removing survival pressure, combat, risk escalation, and game-specific mechanics.

---

## 7.1 Discover

Yuanyuan encounters character patterns, structures, relationships, or knowledge-world connections and feels:

```text
I discovered something.
```

The intended experience is not:

```text
I completed an exercise.
```

---

## 7.2 Understand

Yuanyuan begins to make sense of how characters relate through:

- structure
- sound
- meaning
- radicals
- word formation
- hierarchy
- time
- story
- cause and condition

---

## 7.3 Build

Yuanyuan turns understanding into a personal sense of construction.

Characters and knowledge become building materials for a personal world.

The product should create a feeling of:

```text
This is my growing world.
```

not:

```text
This is my homework list.
```

---

## 7.4 Grow

Growth means more than mastering more characters.

Growth includes:

- trying again after difficulty
- noticing patterns
- explaining relationships
- staying focused
- recovering from frustration
- connecting knowledge across worlds

---

## 7.5 Discover Again

Growth should create new curiosity.

The user returns because the world can keep expanding, not because a streak, badge, or point system demands return.

# 10. Product Experience Principles

## Principle 1: Discovery Before Completion

AA should make the primary child experience feel like discovery, not completion of assigned work.

---

## Principle 2: Construction Before Consumption

AA should frame characters and knowledge as materials for building a personal world, not as content to consume or exercises to finish.

---

## Principle 3: Relationships Before Isolated Items

AA should emphasize relationships between characters, concepts, systems, stories, and structures.

The user should understand patterns rather than only memorize isolated items.

---

## Principle 4: Ownership Before Assignment

AA should make the world feel personally owned.

The child should feel:

```text
I built this.
```

not:

```text
I was assigned this.
```

---

## Principle 5: Sustained Return Before Study Volume

AA should optimize for Yuanyuan returning over time, not for longer sessions or more completed items today.

---

## Principle 6: Growth Before Achievement

AA should treat growth qualities as primary.

Achievement may appear through literacy progress, but achievement metrics must not replace growth signals.

---

## Principle 7: Positive Literacy Emotion Before Pressure

AA should reduce literacy-related emotional conflict and help Yuanyuan associate Chinese characters with curiosity, mastery, and creation satisfaction.

---

# 12. Parent Value Model

The parent's role should shift from continuous supervisor to light participant and observer of growth.

## Parent Value 1: Lower Emotional Conflict

AA should reduce the need for repeated reminders, pressure, and supervision that can turn literacy learning into family conflict.

---

## Parent Value 2: Positive Literacy Experience

AA should help the parent see that Yuanyuan can relate to Chinese characters through curiosity and construction, not only through requirement and correction.

---

## Parent Value 3: Visible Growth Signals

AA should help parents see signals such as:

- persistence
- focus
- recovery after difficulty
- active exploration
- inference ability
- growth mindset

---

## Parent Value 4: Reduced Teaching Burden

AA should not require the parent to become teacher, examiner, or continuous supervisor.

---
